### Design decisions settled (student drove all of them)
- **desc string pool DEFERRED**; `descs` stays inline as `xl_smallstr128`. Student's own
  cache-locality worry was the *AoS* failure mode that SoA already eliminates; `offset+len`
  only trades a fat column for a cold pointer-chase; sole real win (~6.5 MB footprint) is
  trivial and un-profiled. Good self-catch: "thinking too far ahead without a profile."
  ACTION (student): amend ADR 0002 to record the deferral.
- **Max capacity ‚Üí cap `0x8000`**, single `0.7` load factor, no degrade band. Rejected a 95%
  band (linear probing: ~200 probes/insert at 0.95 vs ~6 at 0.7, to buy ~8k accounts that never
  exist). Derived account ceiling ‚âà 22,937 = `0.7 √ó 0x8000`, forced by the `uint16_t` cap.
- **Capacity/dup check lives in `account_store`** (transaction boundary), validated before the
  two-phase SoA‚Üílookup write to avoid torn writes. Nice convergence: this pre-validation makes
  `try_update` unable to partial-fail, which resolves finding #1 (HW Q2) for free.
- **SoA is reserve-the-max, fixed at init** (~22.9k / ~4.6 MB), a consequence of
  reserve-and-bump + stable view pointers.
